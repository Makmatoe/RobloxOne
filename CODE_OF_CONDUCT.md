# Community code of conduct

Roblox One's project spaces should be useful, safe, and respectful.

## Expected behavior

- Be considerate, specific, and patient.
- Discuss the work and its evidence, not a person's identity or motives.
- Welcome questions and different levels of experience.
- Respect privacy, security-reporting boundaries, and the Roblox platform's
  rules.
- Accept a maintainer decision to close, defer, or narrow a request.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discriminatory language, sexualized content, deliberate
misinformation, doxxing, publishing credentials or private data, evading
moderation, and encouraging abuse of accounts, processes, updates, or services
are not permitted.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through an available
private GitHub contact method. Do not include unrelated personal data. The
maintainer may edit or remove content, lock discussions, reject contributions,
or temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Decisions will consider
context, severity, repetition, and the safety of affected people.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, and other
project-managed spaces, as well as public interactions made while officially
representing the project.
