SessionDock 2.4.0

Account ordering and faster batch launches

- Saved account cards can now be dragged left or right into the order you prefer. The order is saved and is also used by batch launch. Keyboard users can move a focused account with Control+Shift+Left or Control+Shift+Right.
- Batch launch still verifies every selected sign-in before closing a running Roblox client, but it now prepares the next isolated account session while the current client, local launch hooks, and selected delay settle.
- Each Roblox authentication ticket is requested only immediately before its client starts. SessionDock keeps at most one account queued ahead and continues to start clients and HandleScope work in a controlled sequence.
- Temporary account switches during a batch are no longer written repeatedly to local settings. Your previously selected account remains the durable selection and is restored after completion or cancellation.
- Private-server destinations verified during batch preflight are reused instead of being resolved a second time.

This release adds no telemetry, new network destination, or bundled integration.
